
# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1. Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone.
2. Do not click on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media.
3. Always verify payment requests before approving them on UPI apps.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all online accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
6. Download apps only from official sources such as the Google Play Store.
7. Regularly update your mobile apps and operating system.
8. Do not install unknown remote access apps suggested by strangers.
9. Always check transaction notifications and bank alerts.
10. If you face any cyber fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal.

Cyber Crime Reporting Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
